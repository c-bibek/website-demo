What is VoIP?

VoIP, or Voice over Internet Protocol, enables phone communication through the Internet instead of traditional landlines connected via public switched telephone networks. With Rise Technologies’ VoIP services, your business can significantly lower phone call expenses, including long-distance calls and call forwarding.

Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) leverages your organization’s network to provide efficient telecommunication solutions.
